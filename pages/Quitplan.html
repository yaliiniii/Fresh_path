Your Roadmap to a Smoke-Free Life

Tips to quit

  • Set a quit date and prepare mentally.

  • Identify and avoid triggers that make you want to smoke.

  • Replace smoking with healthy habits (chewing gum, walking, deep breathing).

  • Use nicotine replacements or medication if needed.

  • Seek support from friends, family, or support groups.

  • Manage stress and cravings with relaxation techniques.

  • Track your progress and reward yourself for milestones.

  • Don’t give up after a slip — keep trying until you succeed.
